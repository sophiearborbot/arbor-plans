🐋 Full Moon May 3 = Peak Whale Shark Day at Gladden Spit

This is the single best scuba diving day of your trip. Whale sharks gather at Gladden Spit near Placencia every full moon in April–May to feed on snapper spawn. May 3 is a full moon. Book the Gladden Spit dive operator immediately — this sells out weeks in advance.

1
✈️ Travel Day
Apr 29 — CLT → BZE → Ambergris Caye
Morning
Fly CLT → BZE transit — American via MIA or DFW, ~4-5h total. Arrive Belize City ~midday.
Arrival
Water taxi or island hopper to Ambergris Caye — Water taxi: San Pedro Express ~75min from BZE (~$25/pp). OR Maya Island Air 15min flight (~$90/pp).
Afternoon
Check in, gear up — Rent dive gear at resort shop. Register with dive operator for tomorrow. Walk Front Street, fish tacos at local spot.
Evening
Pier 66 or Elvi's Kitchen food — Best intro dinner on the island. Grilled lobster, Belikin beer, watch sunset over Caribbean.
2
🤿 Ambergris Caye
Apr 30 — Hol Chan Marine Reserve + Shark Ray Alley
6:30am
2-tank morning dive — Hol Chan Marine Reserve dive — 5 min by boat from San Pedro. Channel cut through reef, sea turtles, massive schools of fish, moray eels, eagle rays. One of the best reef dives in Belize.
Midday
Shark Ray Alley dive — Snorkel/dive with 10-15 nurse sharks and southern stingrays in 10ft of water. Surreal, zero danger. Guide feeds them, they swarm around you.
Afternoon
Recover + explore San Pedro — Rent golf cart (standard transport), drive to Secret Beach on west side — stunning shallow turquoise flat water. Lunch at any palapa.
Evening
Dinner at Palmilla / Cocina Mexicana food — Good local spots, not touristy. Or happy hour at the Palapa Bar (water bar on stilts over the sea).
3
🔵 Great Blue Hole + Half Moon Caye
May 1 — Great Blue Hole UNESCO dive (bucket list)
⏰ Long day — boat departs 5:30–6am, returns 6pm. Bring snacks, sunscreen, motion sickness tabs if needed. 70 miles offshore on open water.
5:30am
Depart San Pedro — 2.5h to Blue Hole dive — Catamaran or fast boat. Sunrise on the water.
Morning
Great Blue Hole dive dive bucket list — 410ft across, 407ft deep. Dive to ~130ft (advanced certification needed). Massive stalactites from Ice Age cave. Caribbean reef sharks circle below you. One of the top 10 dive sites on Earth.
Midday
Half Moon Caye — 2 more dives dive — Lighthouse Reef wall diving, sea turtles, nurse sharks. Red-footed booby bird colony on shore — hundreds of nesting birds, completely unbothered by humans.
Afternoon
Long Island stop + BBQ lunch on boat food — Included in most Blue Hole trip packages. Rum punch on the way back.
Evening
Arrive San Pedro ~6pm — Exhausted but exhilarated. Easy dinner, early bed.
✈️
Ambergris Caye → Placencia — Maya Island Air or Tropic Air, ~45min, $80–100/pp. Or land route: water taxi to BZE + shuttle (~3.5h, $40/pp). Fly for the experience.

5
🐋 🌕 FULL MOON — Gladden Spit
May 3 — Whale Shark Dive · Best day of the trip

🌕 Full Moon = Snapper Spawn = Whale Sharks

Whale sharks (up to 40ft) gather at Gladden Spit every full moon April–June to feed on snapper eggs. This is the most reliable whale shark diving in the Western Hemisphere. May 3 is a full moon. You will likely see them.

Afternoon
Boat to Gladden Spit Marine Reserve dive — ~1h from Placencia. Dive starts ~4–5pm (timed to snapper spawn at dusk). 2 tanks. Depth 70–100ft.
Dive
Whale shark encounter dive bucket list — Gentle giants circling through a cloud of snapper eggs. Some trips see 5+ whale sharks. ~$175/pp. No guarantee, but full moon in May = highest probability of any dive trip in the Americas.
Evening
Celebrate in Placencia food — Whatever you want. This is the night. Tipsy Tuna Bar or a nice dinner at Turtle Inn if you're staying there.
